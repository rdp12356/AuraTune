🔬 THE SCIENCE

Binaural beats work by playing slightly different frequencies in each ear. Your brain perceives a third "beat" frequency that matches the difference. This process, discovered by physicist Heinrich Wilhelm Dove in 1839, has been extensively researched for its effects on:

• Attention and focus (Beta waves)
• Deep relaxation and sleep (Delta waves)
• Meditation and creativity (Theta waves)
• Peak mental performance (Gamma waves)
