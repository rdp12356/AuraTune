# Privacy Policy for AuraTune

**Last Updated:** April 21, 2026

## Overview

AuraTune ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for authentication purposes)
- Display name (optional)
- Authentication tokens from Google Sign-In (if you choose to use it)

### Usage Data
- Session duration and frequency preferences
- Favorite presets and settings
- Listening statistics (anonymized)

### Device Information
- Device type and OS version
- App crash logs (for improving stability)

## How We Use Your Information

- **Authentication**: To create and manage your account
- **Cloud Sync**: To synchronize your preferences across devices
- **Analytics**: To improve app performance and user experience (anonymized)
- **Support**: To respond to your inquiries and troubleshoot issues

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using **Supabase** (PostgreSQL with Row Level Security)
- Passwords are never stored; we use secure token-based authentication
- Audio generation happens entirely on your device - no audio data is transmitted
- We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest

## Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Supabase | Database & Authentication | Email, usage stats |
| Google Play Services | App distribution | None (handled by Google) |
